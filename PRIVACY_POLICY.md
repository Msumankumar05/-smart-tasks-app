# Privacy Policy for Smart Tasks

**Last Updated:** April 11, 2026

## Overview
Smart Tasks ("App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Task Data:** Task names, descriptions, dates, times, and notes that you create within the app
- **Device Information:** Basic device identifiers, operating system version, app version

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Analytics:** How you use the app (e.g., which features you access)
- **Crash Reports:** Error logs to help us improve app stability
- **Device Information:** Device model, OS version, app version

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Camera or microphone access
- ❌ Contact information
- ❌ Photo library access
- ❌ Personally identifiable information (name, email, phone number)
- ❌ Browsing history
- ❌ Financial information

## How We Use Your Information

Your data is used for:
1. **App Functionality:** To store and manage your tasks
2. **Notifications & Reminders:** To schedule and deliver task reminders
3. **App Improvement:** To fix bugs and enhance features
4. **Crash Analysis:** To understand and resolve app crashes

## Data Storage & Security

- **Local Storage:** Your task data is stored on your device using encrypted local database (Room)
- **No Cloud Upload:** We do NOT upload your task data to external servers
- **Your Control:** You have complete control over all data on your device
- **Backup:** Data backs up with your device's automatic backup system

## Permissions We Request

| Permission | Why We Need It |
|-----------|----------------|
| `POST_NOTIFICATIONS` | To send task reminder notifications |
| `SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM` | To set precise task reminder times |
| `VIBRATE` | To vibrate device when reminders trigger |
| `INTERNET` | For analytics and crash reporting |

All permissions are optional. The app will gracefully degrade functionality if permissions are denied.

## Third-Party Services

Our app may use:
- **Firebase Crashlytics** (optional): To anonymously report crashes
- **Google Analytics** (optional): To understand app usage patterns

You can disable analytics in app settings.

## Data Retention

- **Task Data:** Stored on your device indefinitely until you delete them
- **Crash Reports:** Retained for 30 days for analysis
- **Analytics Data:** Aggregated and anonymous; retained for 90 days

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- ✅ Access all your data at any time
- ✅ Export your tasks (future feature)
- ✅ Delete specific tasks or all data
- ✅ Opt-out of analytics
- ✅ Request data deletion

## Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13 without parental consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app.
